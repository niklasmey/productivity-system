## Key Themes in Testimonials

When using testimonials in LinkedIn content, emphasize these recurring themes:

- **Structure and accountability that works** - Participants value the systematic approach
- **Clear, actionable steps from big goals** - Breaking down ambition into achievable tasks
- **Gained momentum** - Stopped abandoning projects, maintained forward progress
- **Great energy and positive focus** - The vibe and atmosphere matter
- **Tailored guidance, not one-size-fits-all** - Personalized approach to each builder's situation
- **Inspired by community and 1:1s** - Combination of peer support and personal guidance
- **Helps prioritize and follow through** - Accountability structures that actually work

---

## Usage Guidelines

**When to use testimonials in LinkedIn content:**
- Cohort launch posts (build trust)
- Founder journey posts (show impact)
- MOMENTUM Program posts (social proof)
- Addressing objections ("Will this work for me?")

**How to use them:**
- Pull specific quotes that match the post theme
- Use first names only for authenticity
- Highlight themes over individual quotes when appropriate
- Let testimonials support your story, not replace it
