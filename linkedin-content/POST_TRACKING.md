## Column Definitions

- **Date**: When posted
- **Post Type**: Which pillar (Founder Journey, MOMENTUM Program, Framework, Lifestyle)
- **Hook**: First line of post (helps identify it)
- **Impressions**: How many people saw it
- **Likes**: Total likes
- **Comments**: Total comments
- **Score**: Your virality score (0-100) using rubric
- **Notes**: What worked, what didn't, context

---

## Key Metrics to Track

### Vanity Metrics (less important)
- Likes
- Total impressions

### Meaningful Metrics (optimize for these)
- **Comments** (especially multi-turn conversations)
- **Shares** (people vouching for your content)
- **Profile visits** (interest in who you are)
- **DMs** (real conversations)
- **Applications** (business outcome)

### Success Indicators
- High comment-to-like ratio (engagement > vanity)
- DMs from ideal participants (founders, career changers)
- Tagged friends (shareability within builder community)
- Applications generated (revenue impact)

4. **Morning Routine** (76/100) - Lifestyle + structure = strong reach
5. **Free Goal Setting** (75/100) - Strong offer, underperformed

### What Works:
- **Framework + Personal Story + Vulnerability** (Getting Hired: 1,300 impressions, 12 comments)
- **Lifestyle + Structure** (Morning Routine: 2,000 impressions, 4 comments)
- **Vulnerable Founder Journey** (Cocoony Sunset: Best hook 25/25)
- **Profile Updates** (MOMENTUM announcement: 5x reach boost)
- **Story-First Cohort Launches** (1,600 vs 600 impressions)

### What Doesn't Work:
- **List Posts Without Story** (Vibe-coding hacks: 3,100 impressions, 0 comments - passive consumption)
- **Comment Hacks** (75VIBES: Forced engagement = lowest score 58/100)
- **Weak CTAs** (Best hooks underperform without clear next step)
- **Off-Brand Content** (Cocoony product posts during MOMENTUM pivot)
- **Pure Offers Without Story** (Feature lists underperform vs narrative)

### Winning Formula:
1. Hook with emotion + specific outcome
2. Vulnerable personal story (failing forward)
3. Clear framework or system
4. Natural MOMENTUM tie-in
5. Strong CTA (DM me to join Nov 17 cohort)

### Content Mix Recommendation:
- **40%** Framework + Personal Story (Getting hired format)
- **30%** MOMENTUM/Cohort content (with story, not features)
- **20%** Lifestyle/Process (morning routine, behind-the-scenes)
- **10%** Hot takes (drive comments strategically)
