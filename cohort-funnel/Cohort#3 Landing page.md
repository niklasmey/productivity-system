## DESIGN ELEMENTS & PATTERNS

### Color Palette
- Primary: Orange (#FF8C00 or similar)
- Secondary: White
- Accent: Black text
- Background: White/light gray

### Typography Hierarchy
- Headlines: Bold, large sans-serif
- Body: Medium weight, readable size
- CTAs: Bold, high contrast

### Visual Style
- Rounded corners throughout (buttons, cards, images)
- Clean, modern, minimal design
- Heavy use of screenshots/real product visuals
- Profile photos for social proof
- Icon-based benefit communication

### Layout Pattern
- Single column, mobile-first design
- Clear visual sections with breathing room
- Repetition of key CTAs
- Progressive disclosure of information
